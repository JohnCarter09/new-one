About me:

This is not Wix, or SquareSpace. I built this website using HTML and CSS. Inspect my code if you dare :) Originally from Arizona, I moved to Colorado in 2009. I was stationed in Colorado Springs at Fort Carson where I was enlisted in the United States ARMY. Honorably discharged in 2012 I enrolled in an International Business Program at The University Of Colorado where I successfully finished my bachelor's degree in 2 & 1/2 years.

It was in college when I took my first HTML & CSS courses online. College is when I really found my love for user centered design


Fast forward a few years:

Currently, I work for an enterprise software company called P2 Energy Solutions where I wear multiple hats in the UX realm. Focused primarily on all P2 product design initiatives including site-maps, high fidelity wireframes, UI prototyping & responsive front-end development. Currently, we use Angular as our UI framework. In the past, I have worked in very diverse enviroments. Everything from larger agencies, to small, nimble start-ups. I am passionate about creating effective user experiences that not only inspire, but educate and perform.
