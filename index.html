About me:

Originally from Arizona, I moved to Colorado in 2009. I was stationed in Colorado Springs at Fort Carson where I was enlisted in the United States ARMY. Honorably discharged in 2012 I enrolled in an International Business Program at The University Of Colorado where I successfully finished my bachelor's degree in 2 & 1/2 years.

It was in college when I took my first HTML & CSS courses online. College is when I really found my love for user centered design


Fast forward a few years:

Currently, I work for an agency where I wear multiple hats in the UX realm. Focused primarily on user personas, site-maps, high fidelity wireframes & UI prototyping - to responsive front-end development. I am very passionate about creating effective user experiences that not only inspire, but educate and perform.
